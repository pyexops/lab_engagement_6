Hike to Devil's Bridge

One of the best memories I have is traveling to the scenic city of Sedona with the famous red rocks. There is a trial called the Devil's Bridge which takes you to this breathtaking mountain that overlooks the Arizona landscape.

Jerome

During our week in Arizona, we traveled to the city of Jerome. This town was on top of a mountain and still looked like it was stuck in the western times. I enjoyed the historical architecture of the city.

A trip to remember

Overall, I had an amazing time in Sedona and would recommend it anybody interested in exploring the outdoors. Arizona has so much to offer with views, eats, and relaxation
